# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Door County, Wisconsin community members in two overlapping roles: neighbors who need
help (food, supplies, a ride, a listening ear) and neighbors who can offer it. Many
arrive on a phone, possibly in a stressful moment. A secondary audience is potential
volunteers and local partners (restaurants, grocers, farms) for the Full Hearts Fridge
food-rescue program.

## Product Purpose

The public face of Door County Mutual Aid: explain what mutual aid is, make it
frictionless to offer help, request help, or join the network (currently all via
email), and present the Full Hearts Fridge program. Success = a visitor understands
the org in seconds and takes one of the three actions. Content lives in Sanity;
site is a prerendered Nuxt 4 app.

## Brand Personality

Grassroots, warm, direct. The handprint mark and giant slate-blue display type do the
talking; copy is plain-spoken and solidarity-forward ("we", "neighbors", direct
action). Confidence without polish-for-polish's-sake — it should feel community-made,
not agency-made.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: gradient heroes, feature-card grids, testimonial carousels.
- Corporate charity/NGO sites: stock photos of smiling volunteers, donate-button-first layouts.
- Government/social services: bureaucratic forms-first feel, dense institutional copy.

## Design Principles

1. **Action over explanation** — every section earns its place by moving someone toward offer / request / join.
2. **The hand carries the brand** — one bold mark plus typography, not decoration or imagery filler.
3. **Nothing between a neighbor and help** — low friction, no gates, mailto-level simplicity until real forms earn their way in.
4. **Community-made, not agency-made** — warmth through voice and boldness, never through gloss.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: ≥4.5:1 body contrast, labeled form fields, visible focus states,
reduced-motion alternatives, semantic headings. Audience skews broad (all ages,
all devices, variable connectivity) — fast static pages, no heavy media.
